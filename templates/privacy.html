Privacy Policy

At SkyHigh Airlines, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

We collect information necessary to process your bookings, including your name, contact details, and payment information. We may also collect data about your travel preferences to enhance your experience.

Your data is used to manage your reservations, provide customer support, and send you important travel updates. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.
